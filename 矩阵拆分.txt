construct a network flow as follows:

V=(s, r1,r2,...,rn,c1,c2,...,cm,t)
s is source, t is sink
r1..rn corresponds to rows
c1..cm corresponds to columns.

A[i][j]=1 then there is a edge (ri,cj) with capacity 1.
edge (s,ri) has capacity d(i)/2 where d(i) is how many 1s in ith row
edge (cj,t) has capacity e(j)/2 where e(j) is how many 1s in jth col

then push a maximum integer flow.

saturated edges corresponds to a solution.
